Cranberry Sauce Recipe

Description

This classic cranberry sauce recipe combines fresh cranberries, sugar, and orange juice for a quick and easy side that adds a burst of tangy flavor to your Thanksgiving holiday meal.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces cranberries
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup orange juice

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Dissolve sugar in orange juice in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Stir in cranberries and cook until they start to pop and sauce starts to thicken, about 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and place sauce in a bowl. The sauce will continue to thicken as it cools.Remove from heat and place sauce in a bowl. The sauce will continue to thicken as it cools.
  5. Enjoy!
